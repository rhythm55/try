Our


Story

In the summer of 2018, two friends came together, each on their own personal journey towards health and well-being. While one aimed to take part in a prestigious bodybuilding competition, the other one strived to jog every day to keep him fit and proactive. Each had experienced their own detours and bumps along the road, but one powerful thing connected them together: Health and wellbeing.
Digvijay & Rakesh, were both positively inspired to incorporating health and well-being into their daily lives. They figured that in the market that was ever-booming with new products and offerings, it was easier to afford a high-fat burger than a greens-rich salad! That’s when they found their calling: ‘Serving the community with fresh food and beverages and enrich them with nutrition for a healthy life’.
And with the foundation of GreePro, they wanted to share what they had discovered. Because they believed that the real secret to a healthy and happy life shouldn't be a secret at all!
